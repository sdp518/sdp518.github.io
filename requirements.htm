Requirements

At the start of our project we were given a scenario describing what was to be the end goal of our project. We have gathered the following requirements not only from that scenario but from an interview with the customer himself.

  • The game must not be too violent and there must be no graphic violence
  • Save and pause at any time
  • Bonus is awarded if PVC recaptures by opposing gang
  • Minigame for when the PVC sector has been captured
  • Gang who gets PVC should be awarded with an additional bonus
  • Bonus mechanism, where at end of each turn gang has captured sector, bonus is awarded. (asked to be creative)
  • Identify strengths of opposing gangs
  • Gangs being uni colleges
  • New gang members can be allocated to held sectors
  • In two player mode, a third “neutral” player controlled by game itself (AI)
  • Neutral can only defend, and will never receive reinforcements
  • Must be based on University of York campus
  • Sections of the university must be split by sectors
  • Sectors can be different sizes and shapes
  • Ideally should identify notorious campus landmarks
  • At least one sector should contain Pro-Vice Chancellor
  • Mechanism for resolving conflicts
  • Mechanism for calculating how many new gang members get each turn
  • Mechanism for giving new gang members
  • Bonus mechanism, where at end of each turn gang has captured sector, bonus is awarded. (asked to be creative)
  • Each sector should be allocated by random mechanism to a gang
  • All sectors are unclaimed at start of the game
  • Be able to convert bonus’ into gang members
